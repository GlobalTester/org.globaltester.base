Automatically Updating GlobalTester Plug-ins

You can automate the GlobalTester Update Mechanism by simply changing your preferences in Eclipse. Therefore, open the preferences window by selecting Window in the main menu and then Preferences. The following pop-up window appears:

Please go to the menu item Install/Update and select the submenu Automatic Updates. Then activate the automatic update mechanism by pressing the check button Automatically find new updates and notify me.

You may customize this update mechanism according to your needs.
